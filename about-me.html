About Me

Get to know the person behind the work

I'm Stanley Makanga

A passionate Computer Science student at Karatina University, specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Data Annotation, and Model Evaluation.

My journey into AI began with a simple curiosity: How do machines learn? Today, that curiosity has evolved into a mission — helping AI systems learn ethically, accurately, and responsibly.

My Mission

Building AI we can trust.

I believe that every dataset labeled correctly, every model evaluated thoroughly, and every bias detected early makes the AI of tomorrow better for everyone.

My goal is to contribute to the creation of AI systems that are fair, transparent, and aligned with human values. Whether it's preparing datasets or assessing model outputs, I approach every task with precision and a strong ethical compass.

My Strengths

Attention to Detail

Every pixel, every word, every tag — it matters. I ensure datasets are consistent, clean, and meaningful.

Critical Thinking

AI models need evaluators who understand nuance, context, and hidden biases. I don't just check outputs — I analyze them deeply.

Commitment to Accuracy

In the world of AI, small mistakes can lead to big consequences. I prioritize accuracy in all my labeling and evaluation work, no matter how large the project.
